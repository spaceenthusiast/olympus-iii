Olympus III

Olympus III represents the evolving phases of Project Olympus, the dedicated Mars exploration program proposed in Jared Isaacman's Project Athena strategic document. This initiative calls for a focused NASA effort on Mars, beginning with uncrewed missions to deploy infrastructure during the 2026 launch opportunity, advancing nuclear electric propulsion technologies, and fostering industry partnerships to enable faster progress toward human missions and a sustained presence on the Red Planet.

This domain highlights the sequential advancement in the Olympus Mars vision from the Athena framework, emphasizing rapid infrastructure setup, propulsion innovation, and collaborative pathways to crewed Red Planet exploration.
